Voting System

Voting will be conducted for Student Representatives. The Main Objective of Online Voting System
is to Conduct Free and Fair Elections in our University and will Eliminate a Single Voter casting
their Vote Multiple times. It allows Students to participate in Elections from Anywhere.

Our Application provides a user-friendly experience that may encourage more students to participate
in the voting process.It also enables the university to gather valuable data on voting trends
and demographics for future planning and analysis.
